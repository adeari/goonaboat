Realtime and Go

Hi everyone, welcome to Realtime and Go. How many people in this room are 23 years old or older? Awesome, I'm the young guy again.

I'm calling an audible.

This is not the talk you are looking for.

As I was preparing this talk and practicing it in front of other audiences, it felt a bit off to me. I was talking too much about the individual packages, and not enough about the core message I wanted to convey with this talk. So I'm calling an audible (sorry, organisers). I'm going to deviate from my abstract. If you were excited about my talk because of one of the technologies I listed in the abstract, I apologise. Please approach me during the conference, and I will happily chat your ear off about them. Instead, I'm going to spend this talk making you fall in love with Go, just as I did.
